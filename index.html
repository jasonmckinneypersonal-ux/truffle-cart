How fresh is "fresh"?

Median 31 hours from the moment a hunter pulls a truffle from the soil to the moment our courier hands you the box. We guarantee under 36h or refund the order and re-ship.

How much do I need for two people?

10g of fresh truffle is enough for two generous pasta dishes or four eggs-and-truffle plates. For a dinner party of six, order 20–30g.

What if it arrives bad?

Email a single photo within 24 hours. We refund and re-ship from next morning's harvest. No questions, no return shipping. This has happened 0.03% of the time in 2025.

Can I pause or skip a season?

Yes. Subscriptions auto-pause when your favorite variety goes out of season — you'll get an SMS the week before it returns.

Do you ship to Hawaii / Alaska?

Yes, but on a 48h chain via Honolulu/Anchorage. We'll flag any extended-zone surcharge before charging the card.
